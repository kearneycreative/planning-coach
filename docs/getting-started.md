# Getting Started with Planning Coach

## Recommended Order

1. **Start with the 1/2-year framework** — get your big picture down before diving into tactics.
   - File: `frameworks/1-2-year-plan.md`

2. **Do a quarterly review** — even if it's mid-quarter, use it to establish a baseline.
   - File: `prompts/quarterly-review.md`

3. **Set up a weekly review habit** — pick a consistent day and time (Friday afternoon or Sunday evening work well).
   - File: `prompts/weekly-review.md`

## Tips

- Don't overthink the 2-year vision. Write what excites you, even if it feels ambitious.
- Your 1-year goals should feel slightly uncomfortable — if they're too easy, push further.
- The weekly review is the engine. Miss it and everything else loses momentum.
