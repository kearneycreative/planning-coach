# Weekly Review Prompts

Use these questions each week to reflect, reset, and stay on track.

---

## Look Back

- What did I accomplish this week that I'm proud of?
- What didn't get done, and why?
- Where did I spend my time and energy? Was it aligned with my priorities?
- What drained me? What energized me?

---

## Look Forward

- What are my top 3 priorities for next week?
- What obstacles might get in the way, and how will I handle them?
- What do I need to say no to in order to protect my focus?
- What would make next week a win?

---

## Big Picture Check-in

- Am I moving toward my 1-year goals? What's the evidence?
- Am I on track for my 2-year vision? What needs to adjust?
- Is there anything I need to recalibrate?
