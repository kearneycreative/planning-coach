# Quarterly Review Prompts

Take 60–90 minutes at the end of each quarter to assess progress and plan ahead.

---

## Reflect on the Quarter

- What were my top goals this quarter? Did I hit them?
- What went well? What would I do differently?
- What surprised me — positively or negatively?
- What habits or patterns are serving me? Which ones are holding me back?

## Plan Next Quarter

- What are my 3 most important goals for next quarter?
- What projects or commitments need to carry forward?
- What do I need to stop, start, or change?
- What support or resources do I need?

---

## Gut Check

- Am I happy with the direction my life and work are going?
- Am I on track for my 1-year goals?
- Am I on track for my 2-year vision?
- What would I regret NOT doing next quarter?
